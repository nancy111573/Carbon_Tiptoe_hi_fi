In BT1 most participants read the result of 26 CO2e, which is the result at the final stage of the task, but did not recognise it is a change from the 24 CO2e from the initial screen. This can indicate issues in both system status and recognition rather than recall heuristics. After the participants read the wrong result, we prompted them to read again and stated: “Try to find out how much carbon footprint the ferry produced alone”. Most participants found it tricky to do task one, as the prototype did not help them recall the result of the previous steps and the detail of each row does not show until the next page.
